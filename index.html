About Me

I was born and raised in Dallas, TX. I lived in Denton while attending University of North Texas wehre I studied Biology and Chemistry for my undergraduate degree. After getting my Bachelors Degree I moved back to Dallas and began working in logistics for a ground transportation company. After working in that industry for five years I decided to go back to school to pursue an MBA and attended University of Texas at Dallas' full-time MBA program. After graduation I began working as an IT Project Manager focusing in software development and just celebrated my one year anniversary in June of 2018.

My hobbies include playing video games, reading sci-fi novels, going to local art galleries and museums, and cramming my brain full of seemingly trivial factoids.

I love to travel and my wife and I try and get out of town whenever we have the chance. In the past year we have been fortunate enough to go to New York , Seattle, Portland, Hawaii, the Dominican Republic, Japan and Hong Kong and we are currently planning our next big adventure for 2019.

I am super excited to be going through this bootcamp and looking forward to all of the awesome things we will learn throught the program.
